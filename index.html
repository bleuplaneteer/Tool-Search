Vyond

What is it & what can it do?

Use cases – When a complex idea can be simplified into chunks, and visual representations are helpful, Vyond might be a good fit. When it is not necessary/appropriate to hire actors to play parts, or when backgrounds/scenarios are not possible to recreate live.

Examples:

  • Using evidence in writing
  • EDU 709 - Welcome
  • SSWO 633 - Visit with Vee

H5P

What is it and what can it do?

Use Cases - Content presentation lending to chunking of info. Very easy to make visually appealing. One of the easiest ways to add a variety of interactive elements to engage with content. Feedback can be prewritten for all wrong or correct answers in interactive Embeds well in LMS even without integration

Examples:

  • From H5P
  • Writing Multiple Choice Stems

Planning considerations:

  • What are the major highlights from a topic or module you want students to take away?
  • At what points will it be best to have students review or practice content with interactions?

Limitations:

  • Not best for narrated stuff
  • Can upload audio files per slides, but not captioning
  • Cannot collect data without proper LMS integration

Screencast-o-matic

What is it?

Screen and/or Webcam recording software

What can it do?

  • Records audio, screen, and webcam (if desired)
  • Has annotation tools available if needed
  • Primarily used for lectures
  • Recording lectures
  • (more advanced) editing lectures, zoom, add highlights or simple graphic elements (add annotation after the fact)

Planning Considerations:

  • Some time to learn the software, then mostly recording time
  • IDS can help with edits
  • Schedule ~1 hour training with IDS, more if interested in more advanced editing (unless you already know SCOM)

Limitations:

  • More robust edits may be needed
  • 25 seats
  • Webcam can move around, but if you aren’t aware it may cover content
  • We need SMEs to send SOMREC files if we need to edit webcam placement
